---
title: "x402 Payments on Web"
---

x402 Payments on Web lets you buy and use credits to run advanced Shinkai Web agents at higher volume.

## What you get with x402 credits

- Access to higher-capacity cloud + decentralized agents  
- Priority throughput for longer chats and tool calls  
- Support for multi-agent workflows that need more tokens  

## How to get credits

1) Open **x402 Payments** in Shinkai Web.  
2) Choose the credit pack that fits your workload.  
3) Complete checkout to load credits into your account.

## Where credits are used

- Running advanced or tool-heavy agents  
- Multi-agent chains that process larger contexts  
- Higher-frequency or longer chat sessions  

## Tips

- Monitor usage in the **x402 Payments** section to avoid interruptions.  
- Pick the smallest pack first to test throughput, then scale up.  
- For sustained heavy workloads, keep a buffer of credits.
