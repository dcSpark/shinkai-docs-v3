Bring your AI agents to the browser and make them instantly useful.

Shinkai Web is the browser-based experience of Shinkai. It lets you create, customize, and run AI agents directly from your web browser — without installing a desktop app or managing local models.

Whether you’re just getting started with AI agents or you’re already using the Shinkai Desktop App, this guide will help you understand what you can do with Shinkai Web and how it fits into the full Shinkai ecosystem.
