A quick walkthrough of the sidebar, Agents page, Chats panel, and key actions.

**Main layout**

When you open Shinkai Web, you’ll see four main areas in the workspace:

**1. Sidebar navigation**

Create a new Chat, Access to Agents,Scheduled Task ,My Agents, Olds Chats, and History from the left sidebar.

From here you can access:

**New Chat**  

Reopen previous conversations, continue workflows, or start a new chat.

\
**Explore Agents**  

View an explore for featured agents, your saved agents, and agents published by the community.

**Scheduled Task** 

Create a new task or view your scheduled tasks (where you can edit or delete them).

**My Agents** 

In this section, you can see all the agents you’ve added, as well as create your own agent.

**History** 

Here you’ll find the conversation history with your agents.

You can collapse the sidebar anytime to increase workspace visibility.

**2. Chat workspace**

The central space where conversations happen: messages, responses, tool results, and file previews.

**3. Top Bar & Quick Controls**

At the top bar, you’ll find the main quick-access controls that help you navigate and manage your workspace. You can find access to your wallet and your credits.
