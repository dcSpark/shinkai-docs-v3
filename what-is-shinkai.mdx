## Key Features

### Local AI Processing

Shinkai leverages the power of your device to run AI models locally, ensuring fast response times and complete privacy. 
By processing data on your own hardware, **Shinkai eliminates the need to send sensitive information to external servers**, keeping your interactions and files secure.

<Note>
Key benefits include:
- Enhanced privacy and data security
- Reduced latency for real-time AI interactions
- Offline functionality for core features
- Customizable AI models tailored to your needs
</Note>

### Shinkai Tools
Shinkai Tools aim to automate common tasks and provide a convenient way to interact with various online services and data.

There are different ready-to-use tools for different purposes, for example:

- **Information retrieval**: Like summarizing web content, answering questions with citations, and searching the web (DuckDuckGo, Perplexity).
- **Data processing**: Tools for converting data formats (JSON to Markdown), running algorithms (Leiden Algorithm), and evaluating mathematical expressions.
- **Crypto & Blockchain**: Tools for interacting with platforms like Aave, Coinbase, and Ethereum (getting balances, sending transactions, etc.).
- **Other functionalities**: Tools for getting weather information, downloading webpages, and summarizing YouTube videos.

## How does Shinkai work?

<Card> 
    <Icon icon="check" size={24}/> Shinkai runs open-source **AI models locally on your Mac, Windows, or Linux device**. 
</Card>

It connects to a **decentralized network** to keep your AI up-to-date with fresh data and verify information through cryptographic proofs. This ensures that your AI always has the latest knowledge, without sacrificing privacy.

### The Shinkai Network

By registering, your device (or node) becomes part of Shinkai’s decentralized network. You’ll **benefit from peer-to-peer capabilities**, allowing your AI to pull in fresh, AI-ready content from other nodes. 

The data is continuously updated, ensuring your AI stays relevant and accurate. And with security at the forefront, only you can control access to your data.

## Shinkai Products

Shinkai brings AI to you through a few key products that make life easier, whether you’re at your desk or on the go:

**1. [Shinkai Desktop App](/quickstart)**

Your personal AI assistant runs right on your computer. It can help with organizing your files, managing your emails, and even summarizing long documents—all while keeping your data secure and private.

**2. [Shinkai Visor](/advanced/visor) (Chrome Extension)**

Browse smarter with our Chrome extension. Shinkai Visor helps you navigate the web, summarize articles, and organize content directly in your browser—making your online experience faster and more efficient.

**3. Shinkai Mobile App _(coming soon)_**

Take Shinkai with you wherever you go! Our mobile app will bring the same smart AI assistance to your phone, helping you stay organized and up-to-date, even on the move.



> Each product is designed to give you personalized AI assistance that fits into your daily life, all while keeping your data safe and private.
