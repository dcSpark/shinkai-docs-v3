**Together AI** offers over 100 open-source models. Most of them come with usage-based pricing, but you can find some models for free, like Meta Llama Vision. Users can also create their own models by fine-tuning and customizing existing ones.  
  <Info> 
    You need to log in into [Together AI](https://www.together.ai/) to generate your API keys.
     
  </Info>
  <Steps>
    <Step title="Generate your API key">
      You can get your API Key by signing up and filling the registration form. 

      A modal with your API Key will be displayed. But no worry, you can access it whenever you want from your [Together AI dashboard](https://api.together.ai/).  

      <Icon icon="copy" iconType="duotone" /> Copy the key and go to your Shinkai app > Paste the key.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Select the model to install">
      Together AI offers over 100 AI models to choose from. Keep in mind that most models are paid-per-usage.

      If a model is not displayed in the dropdown menu, you can select `custom model`and add the model ID manually. e.g., `meta-llama/Llama-Vision-Free`
            

    </Step>
    <Step title="Complete the manual installation in your Shinkai app">
      
      In `AI Name` field you can customize how you want to see this AI displayed in your Chats. 
      <Note> This field only accepts alphanumeric characters and underscores. </Note>
      Click `Add AI`. You are all set! 

    </Step>
  </Steps>
